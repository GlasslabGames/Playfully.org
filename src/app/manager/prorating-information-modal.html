How we prorate:

You are about to change your existing plan during your current annual billing cycle.

GlassLab will prorate your plan based on the cost of your new subscription and the length of time remaining in your billing cycle.

If you are switching to a more expensive subscription type, you will be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the year based on the new subscription cost.

If you are switching to a less expensive subscription type, your account will be issued a credit that can be redeemed when you renew your plan at the beginning of the next billing cycle.

If you have any questions, please email us at support@glasslabgames.org.
