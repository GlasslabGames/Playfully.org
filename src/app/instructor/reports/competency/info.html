Intermediate

The student is able to consistently and accurately analyze the givens and constraints in the game.

Basic

The student is able to analyze certain givens and constraints in the game, but needs more practice to achieve the desired outcomes. The student may need to pay more attention to the particular features of each level (e.g., tombs, seas, special objectives, etc.)
