Intermediate

The student is able to consistently and accurately analyze the givens and constraints in the game.

Basic

The student is able to analyze certain givens and constraints in the game, but needs more practice to achieve the desired outcomes. The student may need to pay more attention to the particular features of each level (e.g., tombs, seas, special objectives, etc.)

Foundational

The student is able to analyze certain givens and constraints in the game, but something something something else.

Preliminary

The student is struggling to identify the givens and constraints in most of the levels played. Student should pay more attention to the features of each level before executing any solution.

Not Enough Data

The student needs to play more levels for us to provide a more accurate measurement of the student's problem-solving performance in the game.
