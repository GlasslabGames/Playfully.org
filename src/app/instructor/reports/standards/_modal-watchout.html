Watch Out: Contradictory Mechanic

In their most recent six attempts to build claim-evidence pairs, the player has created three or more contradictory pairs.

What Now:

In continued play by the student, support her/him in thinking aloud about choices as the game progresses. In the next evidence gathering, claim core construction, or Argubot battle, use the game context to help the student:

  • Talk through what contradictory evidence means
  • Identify pieces of contradictory evidence in the current game level, or
  • Use the contradictory evidence to equip a robot or fight a battle

Use the gameplay context to tease out and change misconceptions about contradictory vs. valid evidence.
