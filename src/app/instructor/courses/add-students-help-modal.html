FAQs

Q: What are the student registration requirements?
A: You need to include First Name, Last Initial, Screen Name, and a Password.
Q: How do I choose a screenname for each of my students?
A: Students will use their screen names to log in. To make it easy and reduce the risk of choosing a screen name that may be taken by another student on Glasslab Games, however, you can create a special naming convention for your Class. For instance, if your name is Liz Kline and you want to bulk roster your 3rd Period Science students, you can use "LK3Science1, LK3Science2, LK3Science3..." and so on!
Q: How do I choose a password for each of my students?
A: To ensure your student information is secure and protected, we require all passwords to have a minimum of 6 characters, with at least 1 capital letter and 1 number. Please feel free to create a standard password that is easy to write down and remember, like "MsKline123" or "Myscienceclass1", and suggest your students change their own passwords once they've logged in.
Q: What happens if a student forgets their password?
A: Don't fret! You can help your student by resetting their password information on the Classes page. Click the "View Student List" button beneath each Class to Edit and/or Remove student information.
