What We Offer

GlassLab Games delivers a delightful user experience covering the most critical learning standards. Our games are as engaging as the games students and educators choose to play in their free time.

Discover Great Games


Designed for students, teachers, parents, and administrators, GlassLab Games makes it easy to find and implement the next generation of learning games.

Make Learning Visible


Ever wonder what learning is happening when students are playing a digital game? Our assessment is embedded in the game experience and is reflected back to teachers and students as feedback to empower further learning.

Impact Student Outcomes


The GlassLab Games learning analytics engine makes valid, reliable inferences about student learning, demonstrating the impact that digital games have on improving student outcomes.
